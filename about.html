Who we are

Agency42 is an AI consultancy dedicated to helping businesses thrive in the AI-driven economy. We specialize in crafting human-centric AI solutions that enhance efficiency and drive sustainable growth. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and the people who use it.

Our founder

Kenneth is an industrial psychologist turned programmer with over a decade of experience at the intersection of people and technology. His unique background allows him to understand both the technical and human aspects of organizational success.

At SpaceX, Kenneth leveraged data science and organizational theory to inform workforce strategy and improve operational efficiency. As an adjunct professor at CUNY Brooklyn College, he taught graduate students about technologies that enable organizations to operate more efficiently and how people, behaviors, and processes evolve around them.

An entrepreneur at heart, Kenneth co-founded TalentDAO, a community-funded research organization focused on optimizing operations for Discord-native organizations. There, he led psychometric research and developed Leo, a Discord assistant for researchers that integrates project and research data using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). He also co-founded Noometic AI, a natural language search engine designed to discover creative talent.

Kenneth founded Agency42 to help businesses embrace the next era of automation. By combining his expertise in industrial psychology, data science, software development, and operations engineering, he offers a holistic approach to AI integration tailored to each client's unique needs.
